Ambassador Duties

  • Ambassadors are selected among a huge number of applicants based on their experience and passion. They represent their institute with the responsibility to ensure maximum registration. They may take the Student Activities Committee of their institute into confidence, forwarding them the invites and asking to arrange transport (and other facilities if required) for them. However, if their request is not facilitated, PiON ’19 team will be providing the transport within Islamabad and Rawalpindi from selected points.
  • Liaison Packages including our official invitation along with posters will be mailed to the ambassadors. They will display the posters at the most visited places in their institute to ensure maximum registrations. Ambassadors will receive packages on *add date*
  • Ambassadors should follow the registration procedure given below:
    Step 1: MANAGE PARTICIPANTS
    1. Promote the event on Social Media and display promotional material on Notice Board in your university.
    2. All participants have to register themselves for PiON ’19 through the following link:
      3. http://www.pionpieas.com/register
    3. Participants will select their campus ambassador while filling that online registration form.
    4. Ambassadors can manage their participants by seeing their registered participants after logging into their PiON web account. Ambassadors can also see their participants, modules, and the total fee to be payed by their team and it is their duty to collect the fee from the participants and submit it using the given payment method before the due date.
    5. If a participant selects the wrong ambassador from any other university by mistake, that ambassador can remove the participant from his/her list.
    Step 2: MAKE PAYMENT
    1. Kindly upload a clear picture of Deposit Slip (In Askari Bank branches)/screenshot of Bank Transfer through your bank APP/screenshot of transfer through Digital Banking (Using Website). Demand Drafts (DDs) and Cheques (self) are not accepted.
    2. Fee will be verified in your login portal within 24 hours of payment.
    3. Check your Ambassador/Registration portal.
