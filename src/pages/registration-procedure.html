Registration Procedure

  • Open the registration link: http://www.pionpieas.com/register
  • Click on “Register” to create your participant account.
  • Fill out the form carefully and completely. All fields are mandatory.
  • Select your ambassador from the ambassador drop-down menu.
  • In case you choose a wrong ambassador, ask the respective ambassador to remove you from their participants’ list.
  • If there’s no ambassador from your university/campus, tick the “Individual Participant” checkbox.
  • Submit the form.
  • Open the “Modules” tab and select the modules you want to participate in.
  • Submit the mentioned fee (base, modules and/or accommodation) to your ambassador.
  • If you are an individual participant, submit your fee according to the payment method: http://www.pionpieas.com/paymentmethod
